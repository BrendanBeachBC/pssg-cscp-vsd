Summary of Benefits Available to Witnesses

The Crime Victim Assistance Program (CVAP) helps victims, immediate family members of victims, and witnesses affected by violent crime. Benefits provided by CVAP offset financial loss and assist in recovery from injuries. This summary focuses on benefits available to witnesses.

In this context, “witness” means a person who is psychologically harmed from witnessing a life-threatening or fatal crime against someone with whom they have a strong emotional attachment.
