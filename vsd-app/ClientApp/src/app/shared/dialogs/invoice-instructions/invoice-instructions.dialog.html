Invoice Instructions

Who may use this invoice form?

Invoices can only be submitted for counsellors that are registered with CVAP. If the counsellor is not registered, please contact {{cvapCounsellingEmail}} to obtain the registration package. You will obtain a vendor number and counsellor registration number once registered.

If the counsellor is registered with CVAP, an error message may display if there is any discrepancy in the information. Please check that the registration number and last name are correct and if the error persists, contact {{cvapCounsellingEmail}} to determine the reason.

What types of counselling sessions can be submitted?

  • Individual sessions
  • Psycho-educational sessions
  • Court support sessions
  • Missed session (up to 3)

What type of invoice cannot be submitted?

  • Progress report (progress reports and invoices for this service should be submitted to {{cvapEmail}} for payment)

Can more than one invoice be submitted?

An invoice will need to be submitted for each individual claimant. However, multiple invoices can be created for more than one claimant or different counsellors working for the same practice and submitted together. Please note that the invoice for each claimant will be forwarded to CVAP individually. You will have the option to exit upon submission or continue and create a new invoice with some information, such as payee and counsellor information, being auto-populated for your convenience but can be edited if required.

What you need to have on hand to successfully submit an invoice:

  1. Vendor number
  2. Counsellor registration number
  3. Name of CVAP claimant (who service was provided to)
  4. CVAP claim number
  5. Invoice number
  6. Date of service
  7. Session duration

If you do not have all the required information to submit your invoice or encounter any problems, please contact CVAP for assistance. Refer to the CVAP Contact Information on the left.
